📖 My Diary

A timeline of my journey, struggles, and growth in coding and college life.

📚 1st Year 1st Semester (2024–2025)
📅 My First Day — August 2024

My first day in college was both exciting and nerve-wracking. I met new classmates, explored a new environment, and realized that IT would not be easy. I had little knowledge in coding, but I knew this was the start of something important.

📅 Learning to Code

Learning programming was very challenging at first. Understanding logic and writing code on my own took time. I struggled a lot, especially when errors appeared and I didn’t know how to fix them. Still, I kept practicing and slowly improved.

📅 Exams & Pressure

Exams were stressful and required deep understanding. I spent long nights reviewing and practicing. It was exhausting, but it helped me become more disciplined.

📅 Friends & IT Week

I met friends who made college life more enjoyable. IT Week was a great experience— fun, exciting, and a break from academic pressure.

📚 1st Year 2nd Semester (2024–2025)
📅 New Semester

I entered this semester more confident. I already understood basic coding, but challenges were still there, especially with harder topics.

📅 Continuous Struggles

Debugging and solving problems still took a lot of time. I relied heavily on self-learning, watching tutorials, and practicing alone.

📅 Group Activities

Group works helped me improve teamwork and communication skills. It wasn’t always easy, but it taught me how to work with others.

📅 Progress

I saw my growth this semester. I became more confident writing code and solving problems compared to before.

📚 2nd Year 1st Semester (2025–2026)
📅 Leveling Up

Subjects became more advanced and required deeper understanding. Coding was harder, but I was more prepared and determined to improve.

📅 Independent Learning

I spent more time coding on my own, practicing and building small projects. It was difficult, but it helped me grow faster.

📅 Pressure

Deadlines and requirements became overwhelming at times, but I learned how to manage my time better.

📚 2nd Year 2nd Semester (2025–2026)
📅 Consistency

Coding became part of my routine. I kept pushing myself to improve even when it was hard.

📅 Motivation

My girlfriend Alshlley became one of my biggest inspirations. She motivates me to work harder, stay focused, and give more effort in my academics.

📅 Looking Forward

I know there is still a lot to learn, but I am determined to continue growing and improving my skills as a developer.

💖 Special Mention

I would like to give a special mention to my parents for their continuous support throughout my journey. Most especially to my mother, who gave me the opportunity to enter college and pursue my dreams. Her sacrifices, encouragement, and belief in me inspire me to keep going even during difficult times. Everything I achieve is for them.
